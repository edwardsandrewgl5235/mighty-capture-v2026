## What Mighty Capture Does

Mighty Capture is a compact local recorder made for Valorant players who want to keep the best moments from a match without uploading video anywhere. As an Overwolf app, it is built around automatic highlight capture so key plays are preserved as soon as they occur.

Its entire workflow stays on the local machine, from storage to playback. That makes it straightforward to review clips right after recording, and the dark-themed dashboard gives you a simple place to sort through saved highlights while the app continues handling everything locally.
